Question 2

What are values that x can take to satisfy the following quadratic equation?

$x^2$ - x - 6 = 0

  1. 2, 3
  2. 2, -3
  3. -2, 3
  4. -2, -3
  5. None of the above

Factorise or use formula \[\frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}\] to find OR substitute and check

Sum of roots = -1
Product of roots = -6
Hence factors are (x + 2) and (x - 3)
